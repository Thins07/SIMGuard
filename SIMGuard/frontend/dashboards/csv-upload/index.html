About SIM Swapping

What is SIM Swapping?

SIM swapping is a type of identity theft where attackers transfer a victim's phone number to their own SIM card, gaining access to SMS-based authentication.

AI Detection

Our AI analyzes patterns in user behavior, device changes, and network activity to identify potential SIM swap attacks in real-time.

Behavioral Analytics

Advanced algorithms track anomalies in location, device usage, and communication patterns to flag suspicious activities.
